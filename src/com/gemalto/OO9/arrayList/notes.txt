ArrayList resolves the followings:
 
  1. Resizing of Array(grows as per need).
  2. Ordered collection (elements in same sequence as entered into the list)
  
  
ArrayList common methods:

 1. add(Object object)
 2. remove(int index)
 3. contains(Object object)
 4. indexOf(Object object)   [indexOf(Object object) => -1, if items is not present]
 5. size()
 6. get(int index)
 7. set(int index, Object object)
